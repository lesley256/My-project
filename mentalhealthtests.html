    • Mental health is just as important as physical health, but it can be harder to recognize when it's not well.
    Here are some signs that your mental health may be suffering:

  • Feeling sad or down for more than two weeks.
  • Losing interest in activities you used to enjoy.
  • Having difficulty sleeping or sleeping too much.
  • Changes in appetite or weight.
  • Feeling tired or lacking energy.
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions.
  • Feeling irritable or angry.
  • Having negative thoughts about yourself or the world around you.
  • Feeling anxious or worried excessively.
  • Having panic attacks.
  • Experiencing hallucinations or delusions.
  • Having thoughts of harming yourself or others.

    • If you're experiencing any of these signs, it's important to reach out for help.
    Talk to your doctor or a mental health professional. They can help you identify the underlying cause of your symptoms and develop a treatment plan.
